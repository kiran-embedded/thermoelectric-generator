THE CHALLENGE

ENERGY LOSS

Modern industrial systems lose over 60% of their input energy as invisible waste heat.

Our project turns this thermal waste into an electrical asset, creating a circular energy loop.

THE SCIENCE

Physics discovered by Seebeck in 1821. Heat flux directly creates an electric potential.
